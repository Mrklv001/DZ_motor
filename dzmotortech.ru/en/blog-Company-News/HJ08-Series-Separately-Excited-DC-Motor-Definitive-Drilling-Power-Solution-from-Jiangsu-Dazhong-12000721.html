HJ08 Series Separately Excited DC Motor | Definitive Drilling Power Solution from Jiangsu Dazhong

  • Wednesday, 29 April 2026
  • 0
  • 36
  • 0
As a leading Chinese manufacturer of oilfield drilling motors, Jiangsu Dazhong has engineered the HJ08 series specifically to overcome the one-size-fits-all limitations of conventional general-purpose motors. Purpose-built for the harsh operating conditions of oilfield electric drive drilling rigs, this series delivers application-specific performance, precision control and extreme reliability for tailored power solutions for global oilfield drilling operations. Its performance is validated by extensive deployment across benchmark customer projects worldwide, confirming Jiangsu Dazhong’s technical expertise and product value.

I. Precision Speed Regulation to IEC & EN Standards, Eliminating Drilling Accuracy Challenges



Unlike general-purpose motors, the HJ08 series is purpose-built for core equipment on DC electric drive drilling rigs, including mud pumps, rotary tables and drawworks, to address the complex operating demands of field drilling. Its separate excitation topology enables wide-range smooth stepless speed regulation up to 1:1000, with constant-power speed regulation across the full drilling cycle. From high-torque low-speed operation during drill pipe startup, to stable speed and pressure control during hard rock drilling, to high-speed light-load operation during rapid tripping, speed fluctuation is strictly controlled to within ±0.2%. The series fully complies with European Standards EN 60034-1 and EN 61800-1, with key dimensional tolerances aligned to ISO 286-2 IT7 tolerance grade.



In a drilling project at Sinopec Shengli Oilfield Dongxin Oil Production Plant, HJ08 motors paired with ZJ50 drilling rigs as mud pump power sources delivered 365 days of continuous failure-free operation in high-temperature, sandy field conditions, with speed stability consistently maintained within ±0.15%. Compared with the previously used standard motors, the solution delivered a 22% improvement in drilling efficiency, eliminating historical issues of excessive speed fluctuation and insufficient drilling accuracy during hard rock drilling, and earning formal recognition from the oil production plant.

II. High Dynamic Performance for Extreme Operating Conditions, Enabling Localized Replacement of Imported Equipment



The HJ08 series delivers a rated power of 800 kW, with full torque output at zero speed and a peak starting torque of up to 3800 N·m to instantly break static friction and drive heavy drilling tools. For extreme conditions such as pipe sticking and directional drilling, it provides stable short-time overload capacity of 2.5 to 4 times rated torque, with a dynamic response time ≤80 ms. Seamless acceleration, deceleration and forward/reverse switching eliminates the insufficient power and slow response issues common to conventional motors, enabling more precise and efficient drilling operations.

In a water injection pump supporting project at Changqing Oilfield, the HJ08 motor’s superior overload capacity successfully mitigated multiple extreme pipe sticking events, delivering consistent 3.2 times rated torque to free the drill string without damage to motor components. Compared with similar imported motors, the solution delivered a 30% faster response during stuck pipe events, significantly reducing drilling downtime and associated financial losses, making it the core solution for Changqing Oilfield’s localized import replacement program.

III. Exclusive Manufacturing Processes for Unrivaled Field-Proven Performance

To ensure reliable operation in the harsh conditions of oilfield drilling, Jiangsu Dazhong has developed two exclusive core manufacturing processes for the HJ08 series:
1.Main Pole Coil Vacuum Hot-Press Molding Process: Utilizing premium-grade Kapton insulation material, cured via vacuum hot pressing at 160°C and 3 MPa. This process increases the compactness of the coil insulation layer by 40%, delivers an H-class (180°C) temperature rating, and provides corona resistance and aging performance far exceeding industry standards.
2.Commutator Spiral Groove & Angled Brush Holder Assembly Process: Combined with a high-strength steel wire binding structure, this process controls commutator runout to ≤0.02 mm, improves brush wear uniformity by 60%, enables 18,000 hours of continuous trouble-free field operation, and extends the maintenance-free interval by 50% compared with similar products.
These process advantages have been fully validated in long-term operation at Sinopec Shengli Oilfield Xianhe Oil Production Plant. HJ08 motors deployed at the facility delivered 15,000 hours of continuous maintenance-free operation under 18 hours of daily high-intensity operation, in heavy oil contamination and dust conditions. Brush wear was only 40% of that of comparable products, with insulation performance remaining fully intact. Compared with previously used motors, the solution reduced annual maintenance costs by approximately 80,000 RMB and cut equipment failure rates by 75%, demonstrating Jiangsu Dazhong’s manufacturing expertise with verifiable field data.

IV. Rugged Environmental Protection & Safety Compliance for Global Extreme Operating Conditions

The HJ08 series features a positive pressure explosion-proof design with an IP55 ingress protection rating. The frame is fabricated from 16 mm thick welded steel plate, hydrostatically tested to 2.5 MPa, to withstand extreme environments including desert sandstorms, marine salt spray, oilfield oil contamination, and high temperature and humidity. It is equipped with dual PT100 temperature sensors, a directional speed measurement module, and a 3-tier overload, overcurrent and over-temperature protection system, providing real-time operating status monitoring with a fault response time ≤50 ms. The series is fully compliant with the EU ATEX Explosive Atmospheres Directive, delivering world-class safety performance.

In a desert drilling project for a Middle East oilfield, HJ08 motors maintained stable operation in harsh 50°C high-temperature and heavy sandstorm conditions. The protective structure effectively blocked sand and dust ingress, while the temperature monitoring system accurately detected and alerted on 3 minor overheating events, preventing equipment damage. Compared with other motor brands used locally, the HJ08 series delivered a 60% reduction in failure rate, successfully adapting to extreme desert conditions. This project has become a benchmark case for Jiangsu Dazhong’s overseas motor exports, validating the product’s superior quality and compliance with international explosion-proof standards.

V. High-Efficiency Operation & Cost Optimization for Global Drilling Applications

The HJ08 series delivers a rated efficiency ≥93.5%, delivering annual energy savings of over 120,000 kWh compared with standard drilling motors. Integrated dynamic braking technology converts the potential energy of the descending drill string into electrical energy for regenerative feedback to the grid, delivering smooth, impact-free braking and reducing mechanical wear by 70%. Its mounting dimensions are fully interchangeable with globally mainstream drilling motors, enabling direct drop-in replacement of imported equivalents, reducing procurement costs by 30% with high spare parts versatility. The series is fully compatible with various electric drive drilling rigs including ZJ50 and ZJ70 models, with successful deployment across benchmark projects for CNPC and Sinopec, and exports to more than 60 countries and regions including the Middle East, Russia and Europe.

In an energy-saving retrofit project at a Shandong oilfield, replacement of original imported motors with HJ08 units delivered annual energy savings of 132,000 kWh per motor. At an industrial electricity price of 0.8 RMB/kWh, this translates to annual electricity cost savings of 105,600 RMB per unit for the customer. Full compatibility with the original equipment interface enabled the full replacement process to be completed in just 24 hours, significantly reducing equipment shutdown and retrofit time, delivering dual benefits of energy efficiency and high performance.

About Jiangsu Dazhong Motor

Jiangsu Dazhong Motor is built on a core ethos of technological innovation and uncompromising quality. From R&D and design to intelligent manufacturing, every step adheres to IEC, EN, GB and other global and national standards, delivering world-class drilling power solutions that lead the domestic market. As an accredited supplier to CNPC and Sinopec, the company has become a core driver of localized import replacement for oilfield drilling motors, backed by deep R&D expertise, exclusive manufacturing advantages and widespread recognition from benchmark customers. The HJ08 series separately excited DC motor is more than just a power unit: it embodies Jiangsu Dazhong’s technical expertise and industrial commitment to solving drilling challenges with precise power, safeguarding operational safety with extreme reliability, and driving efficient, safe and sustainable development of the global oilfield drilling industry.
