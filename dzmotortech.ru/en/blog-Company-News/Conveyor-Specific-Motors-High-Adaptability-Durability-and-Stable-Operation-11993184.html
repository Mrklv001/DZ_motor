Conveyor-Specific Motors | High Adaptability, Durability and Stable Operation

  • Wednesday, 01 April 2026
  • 0
  • 85
  • 0
As a professional Chinese supplier and manufacturer, Dazhong Electric Motor Tech focuses on conveyor/belt conveyor operating scenarios, specializing in high quality, energy efficient brake-type industrial motors and servo drive motors. We provide full-scenario adaptive solutions to help enterprises optimize equipment operation, simplify installation processes, extend equipment service life, deepen global partnerships, and deliver reliable power support to conveyor users in various industries worldwide.

I. Analysis of Operating Requirements: Motor Application Requirements for Conveyors/Belt Conveyors

Conveyors and belt conveyors are widely used in multiple industries such as logistics and warehousing, mining, chemical production, and port transportation. Their operating environments are complex and diverse, often requiring continuous operation under conditions such as heavy load, high-frequency start-stop, dense dust, and high humidity. During long-term operation, motors are prone to abnormal noise, high temperature, slow braking, and phenomena such as belt deviation and material slipping caused by insufficient drive precision. These issues not only affect the continuity of the production process but also increase equipment maintenance costs and downtime losses.

With years of experience in the industry, Dazhong Electric Motor Tech focuses on customized production, combining the operating characteristics of different conveyor specifications and scenarios to create brake-type industrial motors and servo drive motors suitable for various conveyors. These products balance installation convenience, operational durability, and speed accuracy, optimizing operational experience from the equipment source and helping enterprises improve production efficiency.

Relying on a mature production system, advanced processing equipment, and strict quality control standards, all products have passed multiple international quality certifications. They can be flexibly adapted to conveyors in industries such as logistics, mining, and chemical engineering, meeting both the practical requirements of industrial production and the economic needs of enterprises, achieving the dual goals of efficient operation and cost savings.

II. Product Matrix: High-Durability Solutions Adapted to Conveyor Scenarios

2.1 Brake-Type Industrial Motors: Safe and Efficient for Heavy-Load Start-Stop



We offer a full range of brake motors in various power levels and installation methods, suitable for the power needs of different conveyors. The key model 40D Brake Motor features excellent braking performance, making it particularly suitable for high-frequency start-stop scenarios of medium and heavy-duty conveyors and belt conveyors. It can quickly achieve stop braking, effectively preventing material sliding and equipment inertia damage during shutdown. The motor is designed with energy efficiency optimization, reducing energy consumption by 15% to 20% compared with traditional similar products. Long-term continuous operation can significantly reduce enterprise energy costs and help enterprises achieve green production.

The products adopt a high protection level design with excellent shell sealing performance, which can effectively resist the erosion of harsh operating conditions such as dust, humidity, and corrosive gases, extending the service life of the motor. At the same time, the bearings are made of high-wear-resistant high-quality materials, with an optimized lubrication structure design to reduce friction loss during operation. This solves the problems of easy wear and high-temperature abnormal noise of traditional motor bearings, extending the service life by more than 30% compared with the industry average, greatly reducing equipment maintenance frequency and downtime losses, and ensuring smooth production processes.

2.2 Servo Drive Motors: Precise Speed Control and Stable Adaptation



For conveyor scenarios requiring high conveying precision, we have launched a full range of servo drive motors, covering various power and size specifications to meet the precise conveying needs of light, medium, and heavy-duty conveyors. Among them, the Circular flange servo motor adopts an integrated flange design, which is easy to install, requiring no complex adaptation adjustments and enabling quick connection with various conveyors. Its speed control accuracy can reach a positive and negative deviation of 0.1r/min, effectively avoiding problems such as belt deviation and material accumulation, further improving conveying efficiency and stability of material conveying.



The DD30_∅30 33W 48V micro servo motor is small in size and compact in structure, with low energy consumption. It is particularly suitable for light conveyors and small belt conveyors, especially for production scenarios with limited installation space. This model balances energy efficiency and operational stability, and can flexibly adjust the speed according to the characteristics and weight of the conveyed materials, adapting to the conveying needs of different materials, ensuring conveying precision while avoiding energy waste.

2.3 Servo Integration and Bus-Type Drives: Integrated Upgrade for Easy Installation and Use



To further simplify the installation process of conveyor equipment and improve the coordination of system operation, we have launched the Servo Reduction Integrated Machine, which integrates the servo motor and reducer into one. The modular design greatly reduces wiring and installation steps, suitable for the power transmission needs of various conveyors. This equipment requires no complex on-site debugging, can be installed and used immediately, effectively shortening the installation period, reducing installation and later maintenance costs, and improving equipment operational stability while reducing failures caused by improper component connection.



The supporting EtherCAT Bus-type Drive (YKB300) bus-type driver supports multiple control modes and can realize coordinated control of multiple motors, making it particularly suitable for large-scale operation needs of large conveyors and assembly line belt conveyors. The driver has fast transmission response speed and strong anti-interference ability, which can effectively avoid problems such as signal loss and transmission delay, ensure synchronous operation of multiple conveyors, further improve production efficiency, and guarantee long-term stable operation of equipment.

High Durability and Easy Installation: Products adopt an integrated design to simplify the installation process and reduce installation difficulty; key components are made of high quality materials, with optimized structural design to improve equipment wear resistance, anti-interference ability, and sealing performance, reducing equipment failures, lowering maintenance and downtime costs, and extending equipment service life.

3. Precision, Stability, and High Efficiency: Servo products feature precise speed control to meet high-precision conveying needs; brake-type products have reliable braking performance to ensure the safety of equipment start-stop; the entire product series complies with energy efficiency standards, effectively reducing energy consumption while ensuring operational stability, helping enterprises achieve energy conservation and consumption reduction.

4. Customization and Full-Service Support: We support customized production, and can customize parameters such as motor power, braking method, and installation size according to the specific model, operating conditions, and power needs of the customer's conveyor to create exclusive adaptive solutions; at the same time, we provide comprehensive services, covering the entire process of selection consultation, on-site installation guidance, and later after-sales maintenance, serving customers throughout the process.

5. Global Layout: As an experienced Chinese supplier, we deeply cultivate global partnerships, with products exported to many countries and regions around the world. We have won wide recognition and trust from customers in various industries worldwide with high quality products, cost-effective solutions, and comprehensive services.
