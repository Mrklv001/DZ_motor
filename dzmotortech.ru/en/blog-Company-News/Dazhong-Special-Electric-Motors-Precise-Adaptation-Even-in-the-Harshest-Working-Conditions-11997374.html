Dazhong Special Electric Motors: Precise Adaptation Even in the Harshest Working Conditions

  • Monday, 20 April 2026
  • 0
  • 37
  • 0
Recently, Dazhong Electric Motor Tech , a professional Chinese supplier and high-quality manufacturer of special electric motors, has officially upgraded its full range of special motor product portfolio. Focusing on harsh working conditions such as high temperature, explosion-proof, variable frequency, and heavy load, Dazhong takes customized production and energy efficiency as its core advantages, supported by a full-chain service system, to deepen its global partnership layout. It provides stable, efficient and highly adaptable special motor solutions for global industrial customers, and continues to inject strong impetus into industrial production across various sectors with professional R&D and manufacturing strength as well as a comprehensive service system.
I. Deeply Rooted in the Special Motor Industry, Consolidating the Foundation for Industrial Development
As a Chinese supplier specializing in the R&D, production and sales of special electric motors, Dazhong Electric Motor Tech has always adhered to the high-quality manufacturing philosophy. With years of deep cultivation in the industry, it has accumulated rich experience in the R&D and application of special motors. Relying on excellent product quality and thoughtful full-chain services, it has become a trusted special motor manufacturer for numerous enterprises worldwide.

We company focuses on the R&D and customization of motors for various special working conditions, keeps pace with industrial development trends, relies on advanced production equipment, a professional technical team and a strict quality control system, continuously optimizes product performance and improves adaptability, helping global industrial customers achieve efficient, stable and energy-saving production, deepening the global partnership layout and promoting the high-quality development of the special motor industry.

Dazhong always adheres to a customer-oriented approach, focusing on the personalized needs of various industrial scenarios, and integrates the concept of customized production throughout the entire process of product R&D, design and manufacturing. Whether in complex working conditions such as high temperature, explosion-proof, variable frequency or heavy load, it can provide customers with precisely adapted motor solutions.

At the same time, the company attaches great importance to energy efficiency improvement. By optimizing product structure, adopting low-loss materials and advanced electromagnetic design, every product features outstanding energy-saving performance, helping customers reduce operating costs and achieve green and low-carbon production, demonstrating the industrial responsibility of a responsible manufacturer.

II. Upgraded Full Product Range Covering Multi-Field Special Working Conditions

The newly upgraded special motor product matrix of Dazhong covers multiple core series. Each series has been carefully developed and repeatedly tested to accurately match the special working condition requirements of different industries, balancing energy efficiency and operational stability. It fully meets the diversified and personalized needs of global customers, further consolidating the company’s core competitiveness in the special motor field and laying a solid foundation for its global partnership layout.
Whether for continuous operation in high-temperature environments, complex challenges in marine applications, or stable output under heavy-load conditions, Dazhong’s full range of special motors can handle them with ease, interpreting professional strength with high-quality performance.

II.1 Exclusive for High-Temperature Conditions: YLM and YE2G Series, High Temperature Resistance & Energy Saving



The YLM series high-temperature water-cooled motors are designed specifically for continuous high-temperature scenarios. Adopting an advanced circulating water-cooling structure and high-quality high-temperature-resistant insulation system, they can effectively control operating temperature rise, delay the aging of insulation components, and ensure stable and continuous output in high-temperature environments such as metallurgy, kilns and drying equipment without shutdown failures caused by heat.

This series excels in energy efficiency: with lower loss and better energy-saving effects than similar products of competitors, it helps customers greatly reduce downtime losses and equipment replacement costs, becoming the preferred choice for high-temperature working conditions thanks to its high-quality performance.



The YE2G series high-temperature-resistant high-efficiency three-phase asynchronous motors achieve a dual breakthrough in high-temperature resistance and high energy efficiency through optimized electromagnetic design and strict high-quality manufacturing processes. They effectively reduce operating loss and surface temperature, significantly extend the service life of insulation components, and avoid various failures caused by high temperatures.

With a wide application range, this series can be widely used in fans, water pumps, general machinery and other equipment operating in high-temperature environments, balancing operational stability and energy efficiency. It meets the high-efficiency demands of industrial production while helping customers cut electricity costs, fully reflecting Dazhong’s advantages in customized production and energy efficiency.

II.2 Customized for Fans: YDTG Series, Pole-Changing Multi-Speed for Better Adaptation



The YDTG series high-temperature-resistant pole-changing multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motors for fans are customized products independently developed by Dazhong to meet the core demands of variable working conditions and variable air flow in fan systems, fully demonstrating the company’s customization capabilities.

Featuring a dedicated design, this series achieves smooth and steady multi-speed switching without obvious shocks. It also delivers excellent high-temperature resistance and vibration resistance, accurately matching the operating parameters of various fan equipment including ventilation, dust removal and air conditioning systems. Easy to install and reliable in operation, it can be put into use without complicated debugging.

Furthermore, this series effectively reduces equipment renovation and commissioning costs and improves fan operating efficiency. Combined with full-chain services, it provides customers with one-stop support from model selection to installation, earning wide recognition from global customers.

II.3 Adapted for Marine Applications: Y-H Series, Complete Certifications & Higher Safety



The Y-H series marine three-phase asynchronous motors are special motors developed by Dazhong specifically for marine scenarios. Strictly complying with classification society standards, they adopt high-quality anti-corrosion sealing and moisture-resistant insulation design, effectively resisting salt spray, humidity, vibration and other complex marine factors, preventing motor failures caused by environmental conditions and ensuring safe and stable operation of shipboard equipment.

This series boasts complete certifications including many authoritative domestic and international accreditations, meeting the requirements of various marine equipment such as ship auxiliary machinery and deck machinery. With stable quality and reliable performance, it provides a safe and reliable driving solution for marine industrial production and further expands Dazhong’s global partnership layout.

II.4 Core for Heavy-Load Scenarios: YZR, YZP, YZP2 and YGP Series, Tough, Durable and Impact-Resistant



The YZR series wound-rotor three-phase asynchronous motors for cranes and metallurgy are optimized for applications requiring frequent starting and heavy-load operation such as hoisting and metallurgy. Through rotor structure upgrades, the motors greatly improve starting torque and overload capacity, withstand shocks and high temperatures in heavy-duty conditions, and adapt to rolling mills, cranes, conveyors and other heavy-load equipment.

Featuring low failure rate and long service life, they effectively ensure production continuity and reduce downtime losses. Made of high-quality materials and under strict quality control, each motor is built to withstand harsh heavy-load conditions, fully demonstrating Dazhong’s manufacturing strength.


The YZP series variable frequency speed control motors for cranes are optimized for variable frequency drive systems. They feature outstanding harmonic resistance and pulse endurance, effectively reducing harmonic loss. With a wide speed regulation range and stable operation, they suppress shaft current and bearing damage, performing excellently in hoisting, loading/unloading, automated production lines and other scenarios requiring precise speed control.

This series has obvious energy efficiency advantages, reducing power consumption while achieving precise speed control and helping customers save energy and costs. Supported by Dazhong’s full-chain services, it provides customers with comprehensive technical support and after-sales protection.

The YZP2 series variable frequency speed control three-phase asynchronous motors for cranes and metallurgy are fully upgraded on the basis of the YZP series. Insulation performance, heat dissipation and mechanical strength are significantly improved, adapting to harsher metallurgical and hoisting environments. They feature faster frequency response and lower operating temperature rise, further reducing the full life-cycle cost of equipment.

Following Dazhong’s high-quality manufacturing standards and customized production philosophy, this series can adjust parameters according to customers’ actual working conditions to achieve precise adaptation, helping customers improve production efficiency and reduce operating costs.



The YGP series variable frequency speed control three-phase asynchronous motors for rolling mills are designed for heavy load, impact and continuous operation in rolling mills. They feature high overload capacity, strong seismic resistance and excellent heat dissipation, withstand shocks, high temperatures and dust during rolling, and effectively ensure continuous and stable operation of steel rolling production lines to avoid production interruptions caused by motor failures.

This series perfectly combines high-quality manufacturing and customized production. Exclusive motor solutions can be customized to match different rolling line parameters, while outstanding energy efficiency advantages help steel rolling enterprises reduce electricity costs and enhance market competitiveness.

FAQ

1.What special working condition motors can Dazhong customize?

Dazhong can customize a full range of motors for high temperature, explosion-proof, variable frequency, heavy load, marine, rolling mill, crane and other applications, covering all models including YLM/YE2G/YDTG/Y-H/YZR/YZP/YZP2/YGP.

2.What is the energy efficiency level of the products?

Adopting high-efficiency electromagnetic design and low-loss materials, mainstream products reach China Class 1 / IE4 or above, leading in energy efficiency. Long-term use can significantly save electricity costs.

3.What is the maximum temperature resistance of high-temperature motors?

The YLM water-cooled type can continuously withstand 180℃. The YE2G/YDTG high-temperature resistant types can operate stably in 150℃ environments, effectively delaying insulation aging and ensuring service life.

4.Do variable frequency motors have harmonic resistance?

Yes. Adopting special pulse-resistant insulation, optimized magnetic circuit and bearing protection design, they are suitable for long-term operation with frequency converters, greatly reducing failure rates.

5.Do explosion-proof and marine motors have relevant certifications?

Complete certifications are available, including explosion-proof, classification society, CCC, CE, IECEx and others, meeting acceptance requirements for various domestic and international projects.

6.What is the delivery time for customized motors?

Standard customized products are delivered within 7–15 working days. For complex non-standard products, delivery time is confirmed based on the scheme to ensure rapid response without production delays.

7.What about product warranty and maintenance services?

The whole motor is covered under warranty. We also provide full-process maintenance support including on-site commissioning, regular inspection and spare parts supply, effectively reducing customers’ maintenance costs.

8.Can products be exported and is global partnership supported?

Direct export is available. We provide one-stop services including customs declaration, certification and technical docking, fully supporting cooperation with global distributors and end users to deepen global partnerships.

9.What is the difference between special motors and general motors?

Specially designed for special working conditions, they have significant advantages in high temperature resistance, impact resistance, variable frequency endurance and strong adaptability. Service life and operational stability far exceed general motors.

10.How to quickly select a motor suitable for my working conditions?
Customers only need to provide working condition requirements, power, voltage, speed, protection class, installation method and other parameters. Dazhong engineers will provide a professional selection scheme one-on-one.
