  • Description

NEPX series explosion-proof ultra efficient three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios

It is mainly applied to drive scenarios in explosive hazardous environments in North America and worldwide adopting NEMA standards, including petroleum exploitation, oil and gas gathering and transportation, fine chemical industry, pharmaceutical explosion-proof production lines, grain processing dust explosion-proof working conditions, and offshore platform supporting equipment.

Product Certification

It complies with NEMA MG 1 standards and NEMA Premium ultra-high efficiency standards, can provide UL, CSA, ATEX, IECEx, and other international explosion-proof and energy efficiency certification services upon project requirements, and has passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, fully meeting the compliance access requirements of North American and global markets.

Manufacturer Strength

With many years of R&D and manufacturing experience in NEMA standard motors, we have built a motor performance test laboratory and explosion-proof test device in line with North American standards. We have full capabilities for independent R&D, design, manufacturing, and full-performance testing of NEMA standard explosion-proof motors. The products are fully adapted to the technical and compliance requirements of North American and global markets, and can quickly respond to the mass procurement and customized needs of overseas customers.

Global After-sales Service

We provide exclusive technical support and operation and maintenance services for explosion-proof working conditions worldwide, and offer installation guidance, compliance verification, and operation commissioning services for overseas projects. We are equipped with a professional international technical service team, providing 7×24 all-weather multilingual technical response to quickly resolve abnormal equipment operation, minimizing customers' equipment operation risks and production interruption losses.
