  • Description

YDTG high-temperature resistant fan dedicated variable pole majority three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios

It is mainly applied to high-temperature workshop ventilation systems in metallurgy, chemical industry, building materials and other industries, fire smoke exhaust systems in high-rise buildings, as well as ventilation and air exchange systems in tunnels and underground garages and other fan equipment requiring multi-speed regulation and high-temperature resistance.

Product Certification

It has passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, complies with GB 18613-2020 National Standard for Energy Efficiency Limits and Grades of Electric Motors. All products have undergone strict fan load simulation tests and factory inspections to ensure that product performance meets fan operation requirements.

Manufacturer Strength

We have a professional R&D team for fan-dedicated motors and rich industry application experience, and have an in-depth understanding of the operating characteristics and working condition requirements of various fans. We can provide one-stop solutions from motor selection, design to manufacturing, helping customers optimize ventilation system performance and reduce operating costs.

Global After-sales Service

We provide professional technical support and after-sales service for fan motors, can assist customers in matching and commissioning motors and fans, and provide on-site installation guidance and troubleshooting services to ensure the normal operation of ventilation systems.
