 Servo electric push rod


Typical Application Scenarios:

Smart home lifting mechanisms, medical nursing beds, office equipment adjustment mechanisms, small automation equipment actuators and other applications requiring a balance between accuracy and cost.

Quality Assurance:

Obtained CE and RoHS certifications. Each push rod undergoes 1000 full-stroke tests and thrust stability testing to ensure reliable operation and long service life.

Customization Service:

We can customize electric push rods with different strokes, thrusts, and installation methods according to customer requirements to meet the needs of various civil and industrial small equipment.
