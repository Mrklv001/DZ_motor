ZJ500-1000 Separately Excited DC Motor for Oilfield Drilling


Application Scenarios

Mainly applied in explosive hazardous environments such as petroleum refining, coal chemical industry, natural gas processing and pharmaceutical chemical industry, and suitable for heavy-duty continuous operation equipment including explosion-proof compressors, chemical pumps, reactor agitators, mine winches and explosion-proof conveyors.

Product Certification

It has obtained the positive pressure explosion protection certificate issued by the National Supervision and Inspection Center for Explosion Protection Electrical Products, passed the ISO9001 Quality Management System certification, and complies with GB 3836 series national explosion-proof standards and IEC 60079 international explosion-proof standards.
