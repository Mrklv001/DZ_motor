  • Description

NEMA D design three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios

It is mainly applied to heavy-duty shock working condition drive scenarios in North America and global markets adopting NEMA standards, including oil well pump jacks, lifting equipment, punch presses, forging equipment, mine hoisting equipment, material handling machinery, and large injection molding machines.

Product Certification

It strictly complies with NEMA MG 1 Design D standards, can provide UL, CSA, and other international certification services upon project requirements, and has passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, fully meeting the compliance access requirements of North American and global markets.

Manufacturer Strength

With many years of R&D and manufacturing experience in NEMA standard heavy-duty motors, we have built a full-performance test laboratory for heavy-duty motors in line with North American standards, and have full capabilities for independent R&D, design, manufacturing, and full-performance testing of NEMA Design D motors. We can provide customized optimized design for heavy-duty shock working conditions, the product performance fully matches the technical requirements of North American and global markets, and can quickly respond to the procurement and customized needs of overseas customers.

Global After-sales Service

We provide exclusive technical support and operation and maintenance services for heavy-duty working conditions worldwide, and offer adapted drive scheme recommendations for customers' heavy-duty application scenarios. We are equipped with a professional international technical service team, providing 7×24 all-weather multilingual technical response to quickly resolve abnormal equipment operation and ensure long-term stable operation of the equipment under heavy-duty working conditions.
