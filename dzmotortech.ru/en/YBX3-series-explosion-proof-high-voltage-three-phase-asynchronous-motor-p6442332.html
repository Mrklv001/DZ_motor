YBX3 series explosion-proof high-voltage three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios       

Widely used in large petrochemical plants, oil & gas refining bases, underground mines, coal chemical industrial parks, hazardous chemical industrial parks and other high-risk explosion-proof zones. It perfectly matches core heavy-duty supporting equipment such as large process pumps, centrifugal compressors, heavy-duty conveying machinery and high-voltage large-sized fans.

Product Certification

The complete machine is equipped with full flameproof qualification certification, high-voltage motor energy efficiency registration and special industrial equipment manufacturing credentials. All technical parameters and safety specifications fully meet the access standards of domestic high-risk industries and large industrial projects, qualifying for deployment in key explosion-proof engineering projects.

Manufacturer Strength

We own independent R&D, structural design and mass production capacity for high-voltage flameproof motors, equipped with standardized production workshops and a full set of precision testing instruments. Customized solutions for special voltage levels, non-standard pole numbers and exclusive mounting types are available to meet personalized non-standard demands of large-scale engineering projects, with strict quality inspection implemented for every single unit.

After-sales Service

A professional technical team specializes in solution support for high-voltage explosion-proof working conditions. We provide on-site installation guidance, equipment commissioning and regular operational maintenance inspection, with rapid after-sales response to fault troubleshooting. It minimizes unexpected shutdown risks and guarantees long-term stable and reliable operation of the equipment.
