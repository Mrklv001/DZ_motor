Wire Embedding Workshop

The wire embedding process in the workshop is crucial for the motor's performance.This workshop mainly engages in winding, embedding, shaping, and dipping of motor windings. The total investment in the wire embedding workshop is 16 million USD, and currently there are 20 fully automated wire embedding equipment production lines, mainly used for manufacturing high-efficiency winding stator iron cores. Automated winding machines ensure the accuracy of the wire gauge, the correctness of the number of turns and the precision of the winding. The daily production capacity of the workshop is 1200 units, and this production line fully guarantees the consistency and stability of product quality.

Immersion Paint Workshop

To insulate windings and protect against environmental factors, we use insulating varnish, epoxy, or polyester coatings. When the stator is immersed in varnish, it can achieve even coverage on complex geometries. Then, it is oven - cured at the standard curing temperature and time to ensure enhanced heat dissipation and mechanical rigidity. We have a total of 26 vacuum continuous impregnation equipment with an investment of nearly 4 million USD. The process flow is one - stop production from pre - drying of the winding stator core, vacuum coating, stator drying, and cooling. The immersion paint production process is fully enclosed internally and produced under negative pressure, ensuring the penetration rate of the product, guaranteeing the paint content and solidification degree of the winding stator core, and reducing the paint flow on the inner and outer walls of the winding stator core.

Precision machining workshop

The precision machining workshop covers an area of 31,500 square meters and is mainly engaged in the production of machine bases, end caps, shafts, and rotors. The company has invested in 200 pieces of production equipment in the early stage, including 20 production units, 6 sets of automated production lines, and 2 sets of automated cleaning equipment. The 20 production units are used for manufacturing machine bases, end covers, and shafts. All production lines have achieved one-time clamping, completing all processes including lathing, milling, drilling, tapping, etc., ensuring the consistency of all dimensional tolerances and positional tolerances. At the same time, it also ensures the reliability and stability of the overall performance, vibration, and noise.

Sufficient Storage of Silicon Steel Sheets

In order to ensure product delivery to global customers, we have stocked various raw materials for over 6 months, including silicon steel sheets, enameled wire, and commonly used materials for rotor shafts. The continuous production of motors relies on the timely supply of various raw materials. We ensure that the delivery time of conventional motors does not exceed 35 days, and the delivery time of newly developed motors does not exceed 65 days.

Assembly Line Workshop

With 24 fully automated assembly lines as the foundation, we've established a comprehensive production system for large, medium, and small motors. Eight dedicated lines for large motors, equipped with high - precision CNC machining and intelligent torque control, can custom - produce heavy - duty motors from 0.37kW to 20,000kW. Sixteen flexible lines enable quick model changes (within 15 minutes) and produce 3,000 units daily. All 24 lines operate year - round, with an annual capacity exceeding 1 million units. Our smart warehousing integrates with a 5G logistics platform for guaranteed on - time delivery. Each motor undergoes 36 strict quality tests, ensuring reliable operation in harsh global environments.

Dynamic Balance Testing Workshop

Dynamic balance testing is a systematic process that combines precise measurement, vector analysis, and balancing adjustment. During the process, the rotor is rotated at a predetermined speed, the vibration force on both sides is measured via sensors, the unbalanced angular position is identified through phase detection, and precise dynamic balance testing and adjustment are then performed on the rotor. This minimizes vibration, prevents premature wear, ensures the motor operates smoothly, and enhances its lifespan and performance.We have a total of 20 sets of dynamic balance testing equipment, ensuring each motor rotor undergoes rigorous dynamic balance testing. This improves motor energy efficiency and reduces noise.

Silicon Steel Sheet Workshop

Our workshop is equipped with a total of 60 silicon steel sheet punching machines, including 12 large punching machines. Silicon steel sheets need to have high magnetic permeability to reduce eddy current losses. In order to ensure the accuracy and consistency of stacking and bonding, we use precision molds for production. These precision molds can produce slotted/toothed rotor stacks for windings or magnets, providing favorable guarantees for the next step of rotor installation.
