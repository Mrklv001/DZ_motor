  • Description

YBBP Series Variable Frequency Speed Regulation Flame proof Low voltage Three phase Motor


Application Scenarios:

It is mainly applied to variable frequency drive scenarios in Zone 1 and Zone 2 hazardous areas with Class ⅡA and ⅡB explosive gas environments, including petroleum exploitation, oil and gas gathering and transportation, fine chemical reactors, coal chemical conveying equipment, pharmaceutical explosion-proof production lines, grain processing dust explosion-proof working conditions, and port explosion-proof lifting equipment.


Product Certification:

It has obtained the flameproof explosion protection certificate issued by the National Supervision and Inspection Center for Explosion Protection Electrical Products, passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, complies with IEC 60079 international explosion-proof standards and GB 18613-2020 national standard for energy efficiency limits and grades of electric motors. Customized ATEX and IECEx international explosion-proof certification services are available upon project requirements.


Manufacturer Strength:

With over 30 years of independent R&D and manufacturing experience in explosion-proof motors and variable frequency motors, we have built a leading domestic professional explosion-proof motor laboratory and full-working-condition motor performance test station. We have full capabilities for independent flameproof structure design, electromagnetic scheme simulation optimization, full-process manufacturing, and full-performance type testing, and can provide one-to-one non-standard customized development for special explosion-proof working conditions, fully meeting the personalized needs of high-risk explosion-proof industrial projects worldwide.


Global After-sales Service:

We provide exclusive technical support and customized operation and maintenance solutions for explosion-proof working conditions, and offer on-site installation guidance, explosion-proof compliance verification, and regular operation commissioning services for domestic and overseas explosion-proof projects. We are equipped with a 7×24 all-weather technical response channel to quickly resolve abnormal equipment operation, minimizing customers' equipment operation risks and production interruption losses.
