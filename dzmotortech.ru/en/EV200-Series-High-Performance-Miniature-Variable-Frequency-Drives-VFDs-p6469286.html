EV200 Series High-Performance Miniature Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)

The EV200 series is an ultra-compact high-performance miniature VFD developed for small equipment and space-constrained applications. It integrates complete vector control functions and comprehensive protection mechanisms in an extremely small form factor, offering easy installation and simple operation. It is the ideal choice for automation upgrades of small equipment and OEM mass production. This series covers power ratings from 0.4kW to 7.5kW, with single-phase 220V models matching 0.4kW–3.7kW motors and three-phase 380V models matching 0.75kW–7.5kW motors. It features an output frequency range of 0Hz–500Hz, with a minimum frame size of only 60×129mm and a maximum frame size of just 73×168mm, weighing 1.3kg–3.9kg—40% smaller than conventional VFDs of the same power.

   Its extremely compact modular design allows direct installation inside control cabinets or on equipment bodies without additional installation space, perfectly solving the problem of insufficient installation space for small equipment. It is particularly suitable for highly integrated products such as medical equipment, office equipment and small processing machinery.

   Built-in simple PLC and 16-speed control functions eliminate the need for additional controllers to implement basic logic control and automatic operation, significantly reducing system hardware costs and wiring complexity, and effectively shortening equipment commissioning cycles.

   Features an intuitive user interface with easy parameter setup, supporting one-click factory reset and USB parameter copy functions. Non-professional technicians can quickly master operation, significantly lowering the technical threshold for equipment maintenance and operation.

   It has provided stable OEM supporting services for many well-known domestic small home appliance and medical equipment manufacturers, with cumulative shipments exceeding 500,000 units and a factory defect rate below 0.1%, earning wide recognition and long-term trust from customers.

Application Scenarios

Primarily suitable for speed control of small power equipment such as small fans, pumps, conveyors, packaging machines, sealing machines, medical equipment, fitness equipment and electric doors, serving industries including small home appliances, medical, office equipment and light industry machinery.
