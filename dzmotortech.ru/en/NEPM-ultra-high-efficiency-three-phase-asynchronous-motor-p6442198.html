  • Description

NEPM ultra high efficiency three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios

It is mainly applied to general industrial drive scenarios in North America and worldwide adopting NEMA standards, including water pumps, fans, air compressors, conveying machinery, food processing equipment, packaging machinery, HVAC systems, and agricultural machinery.

Product Certification

It complies with NEMA MG 1 standards and NEMA Premium ultra-high efficiency standards, can provide UL, CSA, CE, and other international certification services upon project requirements, and has passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, fully meeting the compliance access requirements of North American and global markets.

Manufacturer Strength

With many years of R&D and manufacturing experience in NEMA standard ultra-high efficiency motors, we have built a full-performance motor test laboratory in line with North American standards, and have full-process independent R&D and mass production capacity for NEMA standard motors. The products have high standardization and stable quality, can quickly respond to the mass procurement and customized needs of overseas customers, and fully meet the supporting requirements of North American and global general industrial markets.

Global After-sales Service

We provide full-process technical support and operation and maintenance services for general industrial scenarios worldwide, and offer adapted drive scheme recommendations for customers' application scenarios. We are equipped with a professional international technical service team, providing 7×24 all-weather multilingual technical response to quickly answer customers' technical questions and resolve equipment abnormalities, ensuring long-term stable and efficient operation of the equipment.
