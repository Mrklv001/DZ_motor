  • Description

ZZJ-800 series rolling mill auxiliary transmission DC motor


Application Scenarios

Core applications include auxiliary transmission equipment such as roller tables, lifting tables, pushers, cooling beds and tilting gear of metallurgical steel rolling production lines, as well as drive scenarios with short-time heavy load and frequent start-stop such as hoisting machinery, mine winches and construction machinery.

Product Certification

It has passed the ISO9001 Quality Management System certification, complies with GB/T 755 Rotating Electrical Machines – Rating and Performance, GB/T 21973 Technical Specification for Three-phase Asynchronous Motors for Hoisting and Metallurgy, and meets the equipment access requirements of the metallurgical industry.
