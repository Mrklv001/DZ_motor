YB series motor _YB2 high-voltage explosion-proof three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios

Mainly applied in petroleum refining, natural gas processing, fine chemical, pharmaceutical, underground coal mines, paint and coating production and other Class I, IIA, IIB, IIC Zone 1 and Zone 2 explosive hazardous areas, matching various general industrial equipment including centrifugal pumps, fans, compressors, mixers, conveyors and hoists.

Product Certification

Obtained the explosion protection certificate for explosion-proof electrical products, National Mine Safety Mark (MA) Certification, IE3 ultra-high efficiency certification, and passed ISO9001 Quality Management System certification, fully complying with international and domestic explosion protection industry standards and access requirements of high-risk industrial projects.

Manufacturer Strength

We have independent R&D capability for the full series of flameproof motors and a leading domestic test center for explosion-proof motors, which can realize type test and performance verification of motors with full explosion protection grades. We have mature standardized mass production capacity, can quickly respond to user customized needs, and our products have been widely used in thousands of domestic explosion-proof industrial projects.

Global After-sales Service

We provide exclusive whole-life cycle services for full-category explosion-proof working conditions, including pre-sales explosion protection compliance consultation and selection guidance, in-sales explosion protection installation specification training and commissioning support, and after-sales regular explosion protection performance testing and equipment maintenance services. We have multiple service outlets nationwide with 24/7 technical response, and can provide on-site technical support for domestic and overseas projects.
