  • Description

YE3P series three-level energy efficiency variable frequency speed control low-voltage three-phase asynchronous motor 


Application Scenarios

It is mainly applied to general industrial variable frequency speed regulation drive scenarios, including ordinary machining equipment, conveying production lines, fan and pump variable frequency systems, construction machinery, agricultural machinery, environmental protection equipment, and general lifting and transportation equipment.

Product Certification

It has passed China Energy Label IE3 premium efficiency filing, obtained an energy efficiency type test report issued by a national accredited laboratory, passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, and complies with IEC 60034 series international standards and GB/T 10069.1 national standard for rotating electrical machines - measurement methods and limits of noise. Relevant customized certification services are available upon project requirements.

Manufacturer Strength

We have a mature mass production system for standardized general-purpose motors, and have built a fully automatic motor processing production line and standardized performance testing station, with large-scale mass production capacity for the full series of general variable frequency motors. The products have stable quality and short delivery cycle, can quickly respond to customers' mass procurement and non-standard customization needs, and fully meet the supporting requirements of various general industrial projects.

Global After-sales Service

We provide full-process general technical support and operation and maintenance services, and offer selection guidance and supporting scheme recommendations for customers' application scenarios, with a professional technical team providing rapid installation and commissioning guidance and fault disposal services. Our 7×24 technical response channel can answer customers' technical questions at any time to ensure stable and reliable operation of the equipment.
