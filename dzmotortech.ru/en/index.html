    About Us

    As a world-leading industrial electric motor manufacturer and professional motor supplier in China, Dazhong was established in 1958 with a registered capital of $31 million.
    We specialize in the production of a wide range of high-efficiency and high-performance industrial motors, including servo motors, the mainstream three-phase motor, various high-voltage motor and low-voltage motor products, industry-specific explosion-proof motor, DC motors, permanent magnet motors, other custom special motors and matched VFDs.
    With 2,000 employees, 68% of whom are professional technicians, including 1,360 senior and master-level experts, our factory achieves an annual production capacity of 10 million units or 14 million kW.
    We pride ourselves on being a leading manufacturer of high-efficiency electric motor products and a reliable motor supplier of high-performance drive solutions, catering to diverse industrial needs worldwide. Committed to the principle of "quality first and customer priority", we consistently provide high-quality products and full-cycle services to meet the ever-growing global industrial demands.
