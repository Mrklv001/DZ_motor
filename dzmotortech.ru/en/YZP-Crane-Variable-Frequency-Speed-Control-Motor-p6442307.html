YZP Crane Variable Frequency Speed Control Motor


Application Scenarios

It is widely used in various variable frequency drive cranes, port machinery, metallurgical lifting equipment, construction machinery and other lifting operation scenarios requiring precise speed regulation and positioning.

Product Certification

It has passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, complies with GB/T 21972 Technical Specification for Variable Frequency Speed Control Three-phase Asynchronous Motors for Lifting and Metallurgical Applications. Its product performance has reached the domestic advanced level.

Manufacturer Strength

We have a professional R&D team for variable frequency lifting motors and advanced production and testing equipment, and have an in-depth understanding of the operating characteristics and control requirements of variable frequency drive lifting equipment. We can provide one-stop solutions from motor selection, system matching to installation and commissioning.

Global After-sales Service

We provide 7×24 technical support and rapid response services, equipped with professional variable frequency motor maintenance engineers. We can provide on-site installation guidance, system commissioning and troubleshooting services to ensure the safe and efficient operation of lifting equipment.
