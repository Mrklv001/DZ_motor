How does the YDTG variable-pole, multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motor for high-temperature fans conquer high-temperature application scenarios?

  • Thursday, 17 July 2025
  • 0
  • 298
  • 0

In the high-temperature corners of industrial production and urban construction, the reliability of equipment operation has always been an inextricable technical problem. From the fire ventilation system of subway tunnels to the high-temperature smoke exhaust pipe of chemical workshops, from the heat dissipation equipment of metallurgical kilns to the emergency exhaust device of large shopping malls, ordinary motors tend to be “on strike” under the continuous high-temperature environment, which not only affects the production efficiency, but also may cause potential safety hazards. The emergence of YDTG-type variable pole multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motor for high temperature fan is gradually becoming a “reliable defender” in high temperature application scenarios by virtue of its targeted technological breakthroughs, interpreting with strength how to conquer the challenges of extreme environments.


The core advantage of YDTG variable pole multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motor for high-temperature-resistant fans is firstly reflected in its “hard resistance” to high-temperature environment. This motor adopts the industry-leading Class H insulation system, the insulation material is treated with special impregnation process, the temperature resistance level can reach 180℃, together with the optimized stator winding structure, it can run continuously for 2 hours at a high temperature of 200℃ without any effect on its performance. What is more noteworthy is that some customized YDTG type variable pole multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motors for high-temperature resistant fans can even withstand high temperature of 250℃ for 2 hours, and can maintain stable output in the face of extreme high temperature of 300℃ for a short period of time, which is a performance far exceeding the limit of temperature resistance of ordinary three-phase asynchronous motors. In order to strengthen heat dissipation, the motor adopts a fully enclosed design with IP55 protection level, and through the combination of axial ventilation channels and high-efficiency heat sinks, the heat generated during operation is quickly exported, coupled with the built-in temperature sensor to monitor the temperature of the core components in real time, and once it approaches the threshold, it will automatically trigger the protection mechanism, avoiding failures due to overheating from the source.


In addition to the core feature of “high-temperature resistance”, the “variable polarity multi-speed” function of YDTG three-phase asynchronous motors for high-temperature resistant fans also provides key support for adaptation to high-temperature scenarios. In high-temperature applications, the load demand of the fan often changes dynamically with the working conditions, for example, only low-speed operation is required for daily ventilation, while high-speed smoke exhaust is required in case of a fire. By changing the winding connection, this motor can realize the flexible switching of two-speed, three-speed and even four-speed, so that the output power can accurately match the real-time load of the fan. Take the subway tunnel as an example, when daily ventilation YDTG type high-temperature fan special variable pole multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motor to low-speed operation, energy consumption is reduced by more than 30%; when a fire occurs, the temperature rises suddenly, the motor can be switched to high-speed mode within 0.5 seconds to drive the fan to exhaust smoke quickly, and to fight for the evacuation of people in a valuable time. This “on-demand speed regulation” ability not only improves the operating efficiency, but also reduces the waste of energy in high temperature environment, realizing the balance between reliability and economy.


In practical applications, the adaptability of YDTG variable pole multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motors for high-temperature resistant fans is also reflected in the ease of installation and maintenance. Many equipment upgrades in high-temperature scenarios face the problem of “difficult shutdowns and complicated modifications”, but the installation dimensions of this motor are fully compatible with the traditional YE2 series motors, so it can directly replace the old equipment without the need for additional modifications to the foundation bracket or drive system. In the workshop renovation project of a chemical company, the replacement of 8 sets of YDTG variable-pole, multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motors for high-temperature fans was completed in just one day, shortening the construction period by nearly 70% compared with the traditional solution. In terms of maintenance, the motor adopts a maintenance-free bearing design, which, together with an intelligent fault diagnosis system, can monitor bearing wear, winding insulation status and other data in real time to warn of potential problems in advance, significantly reducing the frequency and cost of maintenance in high-temperature environments. Its design life is up to 100,000 hours, and in the actual application of metallurgical workshop, it has been running for 5 years without any major failure, and its stability is far more than that of similar products.


With the promotion of green energy-saving policies, the energy-saving advantages of YDTG variable-pole, multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motors for high-temperature fans are becoming more and more prominent. In high-temperature scenarios, ordinary motors often have high energy consumption due to low efficiency, while this motor improves the comprehensive energy efficiency by 15%-20% through optimized electromagnetic design and variable-pole speed regulation technology. After a large shopping mall's underground parking lot ventilation system switched to YDTG variable-pole, multi-speed three-phase asynchronous motors for high-temperature-resistant fans, the annual power consumption was reduced by nearly 50,000 kWh, and at the same time, it met the strict requirements of “continuous operation at high temperatures” in the fire protection acceptance. At present, this motor has been included in the energy-saving transformation subsidy catalog of many regions, and has become the preferred equipment for upgrading industrial enterprises and municipal projects.
