Full Energy Efficiency Coverage and Outstanding Energy-Saving Performance — Helping Enterprises Achieve Green Cost Reduction

Multiple Protection Types, Adaptable to High-Risk and Complex Scenarios — Building a Solid Line of Defense for Safe Production

High-risk industries such as petroleum, chemical, mining and metallurgy have complex and harsh production environments, with many potential safety hazards such as flammability, explosion and high dust. They have extremely high requirements for the protection performance and safety stability of motors, and the safe operation of motors is directly related to the enterprise's production safety and the safety of personnel and property. Dazhong Electric Motor Tech has specially built a full-category protection system to accurately adapt to various high-risk and complex scenarios, building a solid line of defense for enterprise safety production.

The products include a full range of explosion-proof motors such as YBBP explosion-proof variable frequency, YBX5 Level 1 energy efficiency explosion-proof and YBE4 Level 2 energy efficiency explosion-proof, as well as YFB4/YFB3 dust explosion-proof special motors. Adopting professional explosion-proof and dust-proof structural design, they can calmly cope with harsh working conditions such as flammability, explosion, high dust and high corrosion, effectively eliminating potential safety hazards, ensuring the long-term stable operation of motors in high-risk environments, and meeting the safety production needs of various high-risk industries.



Among them, the NEPX series explosion-proof ultra-high efficiency motors have further upgraded energy efficiency and operational performance on the basis of the original explosion-proof performance, balancing safety protection and energy-saving benefits. They can not only ensure the safe and stable operation of motors in high-risk industries, but also help enterprises reduce energy consumption and control costs, achieving a win-win situation of safe production and efficient energy saving, and gaining recognition from many manufacturers in high-risk industries.

Strong Adaptability of Variable Frequency Speed Regulation and Stable Operation — Adapting to Diverse Speed Regulation Needs

In industries such as metallurgy, light industry, textile, machine tools and automatic production lines, special frequency and special working system speed regulation transmission are often required in the production process. The adaptability, stability and speed regulation accuracy of ordinary motors are difficult to meet production needs, affecting production efficiency and product quality. Dazhong Electric Motor Tech has specially developed a variety of high-performance variable frequency speed regulation low-voltage motors to adapt to diverse speed regulation needs and help enterprises improve production efficiency.

There are multiple variable frequency speed regulation models such as YE5P, YE4P, YE3P and Y2VP, which have specially optimized speed regulation performance. Among them, the Y2VP series variable frequency motors have a frequency modulation range of 5～100Hz, high speed regulation accuracy, and core characteristics of smooth low-speed torque, no crawling phenomenon and stable operation. At the same time, they are equipped with professional insulation protection design to effectively reduce the loss of motors caused by shaft current, extend the service life of motors and reduce equipment maintenance costs.

Some models have also successfully obtained marine type approval, which can adapt to the special operating environment of the marine field, further expanding the application boundary of products. It provides flexible, stable and efficient speed regulation transmission solutions for manufacturers in different industries such as metallurgy, light industry and maritime affairs, helping enterprises improve production efficiency and product quality.

At the same time, it is specially equipped with NEMA D design three-phase asynchronous motors, which strictly follow the technical standards and quality specifications of the international market, and can be directly adapted to the production equipment needs of different countries and regions around the world.
