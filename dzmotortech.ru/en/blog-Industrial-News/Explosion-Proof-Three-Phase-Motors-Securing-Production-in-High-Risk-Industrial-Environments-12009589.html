Explosion-Proof Three-Phase Motors: Securing Production in High-Risk Industrial Environments

  • Wednesday, 03 June 2026
  • 0
  • 25
  • 0

Production scenarios in heavy industries such as petroleum, chemical, natural gas, and mining are commonly accompanied by complex operating conditions including flammable and explosive gas mixtures, high-concentration dust, extreme temperature fluctuations, and highly corrosive media. These conditions impose stringent requirements on the operating adaptability, stability, and safety redundancy of core power equipment, especially Oil and gas explosion proof three phase motors that operate directly in explosive hazard zones. Conventional variable-frequency explosion-proof motors on the market generally suffer from structural issues such as accelerated winding insulation aging, bearing electrical erosion damage, and seal failure of explosion-proof joint surfaces under long-term variable-frequency speed regulation. These problems can easily lead to unplanned equipment shutdowns, operational safety hazards, and production chain interruptions.




According to public industry data from the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC), the annual global economic loss from production interruptions caused by inadequate power equipment adaptability and performance degradation in high-risk industrial conditions exceeds $120 billion, with over 60% of failures originating from substandard performance and insufficient operating adaptability of explosion-proof motors. Addressing these widespread industry shortcomings, Jiangsu Dazhong Electric Motor, leveraging decades of in-depth industrial motor manufacturing experience, has iteratively developed a new generation of variable-frequency speed-regulated explosion-proof three-phase motors, including industry-leading IE5 ultra efficient explosion-proof three-phase motors and fully compliant ATEX certified explosion proof three phase motors. This product line has undergone targeted structural optimization and performance upgrades for various high-risk explosive operating conditions. Dazhong  can operate continuously and normally in all classes of explosive gas environments (IIA, IIB, IIC) and supports precise variable-frequency speed regulation across the full 0-100Hz range, accurately matching the refined and differentiated control requirements of industrial production processes and eliminating production problems caused by operating condition adaptation defects at the equipment source.



I. Deep Roots in Explosion-Proof Power Technology, Accumulating Core R&D Heritage



Founded in 1958, Jiangsu Dazhong Electric Motor has been deeply engaged in the R&D, design, and precision manufacturing of industrial motors for over 60 years. In the niche segment of explosion-proof three-phase motors, especially Flameproof three phase induction motors which are the most widely used in hazardous areas, Dazhong possesses more than 30 years of specialized technical accumulation and engineering implementation experience. It is a core domestic enterprise with independent R&D, customized production, and large-scale delivery capabilities for the full range of explosion-proof motors. Dazhong has established a national-level enterprise technology innovation center and formed a dedicated explosion-proof motor R&D team consisting of 126 professional technical engineers, including 23 senior engineers and 8 industry experts enjoying special allowances from the State Council, with extensive experience in high-risk operating condition equipment R&D, operating condition adaptation optimization, and project implementation.



Dazhong has always adhered to technology-driven development, allocating 6.8% of annual operating revenue exclusively to product iteration and technological R&D. Focusing on core technical directions such as explosion-proof structure optimization, insulation system upgrading, heat dissipation structure innovation, and variable-frequency operating condition adaptation,Dazhong  has cumulatively obtained 127 national authorized patents, including 32 core invention patents. This complete independent intellectual property system has eliminated the homogeneous design drawbacks of general-purpose motors, forming a unique and differentiated explosion-proof motor technology system that lays a solid technical foundation for products to adapt to the world's most stringent industrial operating conditions.



1.1 Exclusive Precision Manufacturing Processes, Building Differentiated Performance Barriers



Compared with industry-general manufacturing processes, Jiangsu Dazhong Electric Motor has formulated exclusive precision manufacturing process standards for explosion-proof three-phase motors. Through multiple unique core processes,Dazhong  addresses common industry problems such as poor variable-frequency weather resistance, insufficient sealing accuracy, and high operational losses of conventional explosion-proof motors, laying a solid foundation for the mass production of IE5 ultra efficient explosion-proof three-phase motors.



1.1.1 High-Precision Vacuum Pressure Impregnation Process



The stator windings adopt a two-stage high-precision VPI process with -0.098MPa vacuum degassing and 0.6MPa pressure impregnation, achieving 100% insulation coverage without dead angles. This process significantly improves the overall insulation strength and thermal conductivity of the windings, while enhancing their mechanical structural stability. It effectively resists insulation layer damage caused by high-frequency pulse voltages during variable-frequency operation, delays insulation aging, and adapts to long-term continuous variable-frequency operation conditions.



1.1.2 G0.4-Grade Rotor Dynamic Balancing Calibration Process



The rotor dynamic balancing treatment adopts the industry's high-level G0.4-grade precision calibration process, two accuracy levels higher than the national general industry standard. After finish-machining, the rotor undergoes two independent full-dimensional dynamic balancing inspections and calibrations, strictly limiting vibration acceleration to ≤1.5mm/s² under maximum speed conditions. This ultra-high balancing accuracy reduces equipment operating vibration and noise from the source, minimizes bearing friction losses, significantly extends the service life of core transmission components, and adapts to high-precision, low-jitter precision transmission production scenarios.



1.1.3 5-Axis CNC Machining Process for Explosion-Proof Joint Surfaces



The explosion-proof joint surfaces are processed using 5-axis CNC integrated precision machining, with core accuracy parameters strictly controlled: surface roughness Ra≤0.8μm, flatness error ≤0.02mm/m, and explosion-proof gap precisely within 0.05mm. This effectively blocks flame leakage during internal deflagration conditions, which is the core safety principle of Flameproof three phase induction motors, and complies with international explosion-proof safety standards. Each piece of equipment undergoes a 1.5 times rated explosion-proof pressure water tightness test before leaving the factory, with a pressure holding time of no less than 10 minutes, ensuring operational safety in high-risk conditions.



1.2 Full-Dimensional Precision Control, Achieving Millimeter-Level Operational Accuracy




The iteratively upgraded explosion-proofthree-phase motors, particularly IE5 ultra efficient explosion-proof three-phase motors, adopt an optimized electromagnetic topology design scheme, achieving comprehensive improvements in multi-dimensional operational accuracy through precise adjustment of air gap parameters, winding arrangement, and magnetic circuit structure.



1.2.1 Low Torque Ripple Electromagnetic Topology Design



Through electromagnetic simulation optimization and magnetic circuit structure iteration, the equipment torque ripple is strictly controlled within ≤±1.5% of rated torque, improving performance stability by 50% compared to the industry average. This ultra-high torque control accuracy ensures that the motor can continuously output smooth torque under complex conditions such as low speed, variable frequency, and variable load, fully meeting the high-precision transmission control requirements of fine chemicals, precision conveying, and intelligent equipment scenarios.



1.2.2 Distributed Global Temperature Monitoring and Protection



The equipment is equipped with 12 sets of distributed high-precision temperature sensing modules, fully covering the core heating areas of stator windings, front and rear support bearings, and the casing. With temperature measurement accuracy reaching ±0.5℃, it enables real-time and synchronous collection of global operating temperature data. The supporting intelligent control system can dynamically correct operating conditions based on real-time temperature parameters, accurately avoiding local overheating and overload operation. It also integrates bearing temperature warning, winding overheat protection and overload circuit breaker protection, forming a comprehensive safety system.



1.2.3 Independent Axial Forced Ventilation Heat Dissipation System



The heat dissipation system adopts an independent axial forced ventilation structure. Cold air is directionally introduced from the non-drive end, passes through the entire air gap between the stator and rotor, and is discharged in an orderly manner from the drive end, forming a closed-loop directional heat dissipation air duct. This structure strictly controls the temperature difference of the motor's global windings to ≤4℃, completely solving the problems of local insulation aging and performance degradation caused by local heat accumulation and excessive temperature difference in conventional explosion-proof motors. This significantly improves long-term operational stability and durability.



II. Global Benchmark Project Implementation, Performance Verified by Harsh Operating Conditions



Relying on differentiated technical advantages and strict quality control systems, Jiangsu Dazhong Electric Motor's explosion-proof three-phase motors have been mass-applied in benchmark heavy industry projects in multiple countries around the world, covering high-risk core scenarios such as oil refining, oil and gas transmission (where Oil and gas explosion proof three phase motors are mandatory power equipment), mining, and fine chemicals. Through long-term practical operation under complex conditions, the products have verified their high adaptability, stability, and durability, and have been highly recognized by global end customers and engineering general contractors.



2.1 TotalEnergies Normandy Refinery, France: Tripling Equipment Maintenance Cycles



The TotalEnergies Normandy refinery expansion project operates in a Class IIB, Temperature Group T3 explosive gas environment, requiring all power equipment to be ATEX certified explosion proof three phase motors with high variable-frequency control accuracy and continuous operation capability. The project purchased 216 Oil and gas explosion proof three phase motors in bulk, which are fully adapted to core power equipment such as pumps, fans, and compressors in the plant. After commissioning, the equipment achieved more than 18,000 hours of continuous operation without failure, helping the customer extend the original 6-month equipment maintenance cycle to 18 months, directly reducing annual equipment operation and maintenance costs by 45%.



2.2 Vale Amazon Iron Ore Mine, Brazil: Ultra-Low Operational Failure Rate of 0.3%



The Vale Amazon iron ore mining project faces multiple complex operating conditions including 2000mg/m³ high-concentration dust, high temperature and humidity, and large fluctuations in material impact load. The project is equipped with 158 explosion-proofthree-phase motors applied to core production links such as ore conveying and crushing and screening. Equipped with exclusive dust-proof sealing, anti-corrosion coating and load adaptation optimization, it reliably operates in these harsh conditions. After two years of normal high-intensity operation, the overall failure rate of the equipment is only 0.3%, far below the industry average, ensuring large-scale and continuous production operations of the mine.



2.3 Gazprom Siberia Gas Compressor Station, Russia: Direct Start-Up at -40℃ Without Preheating




The Gazprom Siberia natural gas compressor station project, a typical application scenario for Oil and gas explosion proof three phase motors, faces the stringent requirements of direct start-up at -40℃ extreme low temperature and long-term continuous operation in low temperature environments. Conventional motors are prone to start-up failure, lubrication failure, and insulation performance degradation under such conditions. The project is equipped with 92 explosion-proof three-phase motors with customized low-temperature lubrication systems and low-temperature-resistant insulating materials. They can achieve direct start-up and stable operation in extremely cold environments without preheating equipment, with zero start-up failures or operational abnormalities through multiple extreme winters.
