Energy-efficient low-voltage motors: A new green power option with low energy consumption and long service life

  • Monday, 25 August 2025
  • 0
  • 156
  • 0
As the ‘dual carbon’ goals are being vigorously pursued, demand for energy-efficient equipment continues to rise in both industrial and residential sectors. Energy-efficient low-voltage motors, as core drive equipment, are emerging as a key force driving green transformation thanks to their dual advantages of low energy consumption and long service life. Compared to traditional motors, energy-efficient low-voltage motors not only significantly reduce energy consumption but also extend service life through optimised design, thereby lowering overall costs for users. They are rapidly gaining widespread adoption in factory workshops, commercial buildings, and even residential settings.

The ‘low-consumption’ characteristics of energy-efficient low-voltage motors stem from strict adherence to high-efficiency standards and technological breakthroughs. Currently, mainstream energy-efficient low-voltage motors all meet IE3 or higher energy efficiency ratings, with some high-end products achieving the IE4 ultra-high-efficiency standard. For example, a 11kW energy-efficient low-voltage motor can reduce annual electricity consumption by approximately 2,500 kWh compared to a traditional IE1-rated motor under continuous operation. This advantage is particularly evident in industrial applications, such as circulating pumps in chemical plants or conveyor belts in food factories, which require prolonged operation. Replacing these with energy-efficient low-voltage motors can reduce annual electricity costs by 15% to 22%, resulting in significant long-term operational cost savings for businesses.

Long service life is another key feature that appeals to users. In terms of design, energy-efficient low-voltage motors use high-temperature-resistant special insulation winding materials to effectively slow down winding ageing. They are also equipped with precision bearings with enhanced sealing performance to prevent dust and moisture intrusion, thereby reducing mechanical wear. Industry data shows that the average service life of high-quality energy-efficient low-voltage motors can reach 12–15 years, far exceeding the 8–10-year service life of traditional motors. For commercial central air conditioning fans and residential water supply pumps that operate 24/7, the long service life of energy-efficient low-voltage motors can significantly reduce the number of shutdowns for maintenance, avoiding production interruptions or inconveniences caused by equipment failures.

Actual application cases also validate the value of energy-efficient low-voltage motors. Last year, Jiangsu Dazhong Motor replaced all 30 traditional drive motors with energy-efficient low-voltage motors to power spinning machines and fan equipment. After one year of operation, the factory not only saw a 19% reduction in overall electricity consumption, saving over 400,000 yuan in electricity costs, but also experienced no common faults such as bearing damage or winding overheating in this batch of energy-efficient low-voltage motors, resulting in an 80% reduction in maintenance costs compared to previous years. ‘Energy-saving low-voltage motors not only save electricity but also reduce maintenance hassles. We expect to recoup the initial investment within two years,’ said the factory's equipment manager, echoing the sentiments of many users.

Today, the application scope of energy-saving low-voltage motors continues to expand. Beyond industrial applications, they are now being adopted in new household appliances like washing machines and variable-frequency air conditioners, commercial applications such as escalators and shopping mall ventilation systems, and even auxiliary power units in electric vehicles. As energy efficiency standards continue to evolve and intelligent control technologies are integrated, energy-efficient low-voltage motors will achieve a combination of ‘low energy consumption, long lifespan,’ and ‘intelligence,’ providing more reliable power support for green development across industries and becoming the mainstream choice in the future motor market.
