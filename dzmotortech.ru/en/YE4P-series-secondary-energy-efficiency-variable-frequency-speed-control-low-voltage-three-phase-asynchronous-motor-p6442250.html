  • Description

YE4P series secondary energy efficiency variable frequency speed control low-voltage three-phase asynchronous motor


Application Scenarios

It is mainly applied to general industrial variable frequency speed regulation drive scenarios, including light industry production line conveying equipment, food processing machinery, packaging machinery, plastic machinery, HVAC variable frequency systems, municipal landscape water supply and drainage, agricultural irrigation variable frequency pump sets, and small lifting equipment.

Product Certification

It has passed China Energy Label IE4 super-premium efficiency filing, obtained an energy efficiency type test report issued by a national accredited laboratory, passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, and complies with IEC 60034-30-2 IE3 premium international energy efficiency standard. Customized CE, CU-TR, and other international certification services are available upon export requirements.

Manufacturer Strength

We have a mature R&D and manufacturing system for general variable frequency motors, and have built a fully automatic motor production line and standardized performance test station, with mass production capacity for the full series of variable frequency motors from micro power to high power. The products have high standardization and strong interchangeability, can quickly respond to customers' mass procurement and non-standard customization needs, and fully meet the supporting requirements of various general industrial projects.

Global After-sales Service

We provide full-process technical support and operation and maintenance services for general industrial scenarios, and offer adapted drive scheme recommendations for customers' application scenarios, with a professional technical team providing rapid installation and commissioning guidance and fault disposal services. Our 7×24 technical response channel can answer customers' technical questions at any time to ensure stable operation of the equipment.
