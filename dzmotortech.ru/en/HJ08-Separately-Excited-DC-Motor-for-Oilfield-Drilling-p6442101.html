HJ08 Separately Excited DC Motor for Oilfield Drilling

Application Scenarios

Mainly applied in high-precision drive scenarios in explosive hazardous environments, such as fine chemical reactors, special military equipment, nuclear power auxiliary systems, aerospace test equipment, high-end pharmaceutical sterile explosion-proof production lines, and explosion-proof equipment for semiconductor manufacturing.

Product Certification

It has obtained the positive pressure explosion protection certificate issued by the National Supervision and Inspection Center for Explosion Protection Electrical Products, passed the ISO9001 Quality Management System certification and GJB military quality management system certification, complies with IEC 60079 international explosion-proof standards, and can provide customized nuclear power equipment qualification certification services.

Manufacturer Strength

With more than 30 years of independent R&D and manufacturing experience in medium and high voltage DC motors and explosion-proof motors, we have built a leading domestic full-performance test station for high-voltage motors and a specialized explosion-proof motor laboratory. We have independent R&D, customized design, and full-process production and manufacturing capabilities for the full series of motors, and can provide one-to-one non-standard customized services for special voltages, special mounting types, and extreme working conditions, fully meeting the personalized needs of domestic and foreign high-end industrial projects.

Global After-sales Service

We provide exclusive technical support for the full range of industrial scenarios, customized operation and maintenance solutions for explosion-proof, high-risk and remote working conditions. We can provide on-site installation guidance and regular operation commissioning services for domestic and overseas projects, as well as 24/7 all-weather technical response to quickly solve equipment operation failures and minimize customers' equipment operation risks and production shutdown losses.
